University of Virginia

The University of Virginia (UVA) is a prestigious public university located in Charlottesville, Virginia.

Student Population

As of 2023, UVA has a student population of approximately 25,000, including undergraduate and graduate students.

Popular Majors and Degrees

Popular majors include Economics, Biology, Commerce, and Political Science. UVA is also known for its distinguished Law and Business schools.

Location

Located in Charlottesville, Virginia, the campus is known for its historic architecture, including the Rotunda, designed by Thomas Jefferson.

Mascot

The mascot of UVA is "Cavalier," representing the university’s sports teams, known as the Virginia Cavaliers.

Additional Information

UVA is a member of the prestigious Association of American Universities and is ranked among the top public universities in the nation.
