College of William & Mary

The College of William & Mary is the second-oldest college in the United States, located in Williamsburg, Virginia.

Student Population

William & Mary has around 9,000 students, including both undergraduate and graduate students.

Popular Majors and Degrees

Popular majors include Business, International Relations, Government, and History. It also offers a highly respected Law program.

Location

The university is located in the historic town of Williamsburg, known for its colonial history and close proximity to national landmarks.

Mascot

The mascot is the "Tribe," reflecting the school's unique sports tradition.

Additional Information

William & Mary is known for its rigorous academics, historic campus, and emphasis on public service.
